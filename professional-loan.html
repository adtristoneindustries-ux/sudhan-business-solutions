Professional Loan Process – Step by Step

1

Loan Application

Submit your application with professional details, practice information, and loan requirement. Our team identifies the best lender for your profession.

2

Professional Credential Verification

Lender verifies your degree certificate, professional license, and practice certificate to confirm your professional status and eligibility.

3

Income & Financial Assessment

Evaluation of ITR, bank statements, and practice income to assess repayment capacity and determine the eligible loan amount.

4

Credit Score Check

CIBIL score and credit history reviewed. Professionals with good credit scores get preferential interest rates and higher loan amounts.

5

Loan Sanction

Sanction letter issued with approved loan amount, competitive interest rate, flexible tenure, and EMI schedule tailored for professionals.

6

Agreement & Disbursement

Loan agreement signed and funds disbursed to your account within 3–5 working days. Invest in your practice and grow your career!
