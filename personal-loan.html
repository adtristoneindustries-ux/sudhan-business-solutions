Personal Loan Process

1

Application Submission

Submit your loan application with required documents online or visit our office. Our team will verify all details and guide you through the process.

2

Document Verification

Our experts will verify your documents including identity proof, address proof, income proof, and employment details to ensure accuracy.

3

Eligibility Assessment

We assess your eligibility based on income, credit score, employment stability, and repayment capacity to determine the loan amount.

4

Loan Approval

Once approved, you'll receive a loan sanction letter with details about the loan amount, interest rate, tenure, and EMI amount.

5

Agreement Signing

Review and sign the loan agreement along with necessary legal documents. Our team will explain all terms and conditions clearly.

6

Loan Disbursement

The loan amount will be disbursed directly to your bank account within 24-48 hours after agreement signing. Start using your funds immediately!
