Car Loan Process – Step by Step

1

Choose Your Car

Select your preferred new or used car and get a quotation or proforma invoice from the dealer. Our team helps you compare loan options across lenders.

2

Loan Application

Submit your loan application with KYC documents, income proof, and car quotation. We handle the paperwork and submit to the best-suited lender.

3

Credit & Income Verification

Lender verifies your CIBIL score, income documents, and employment details to assess your repayment capacity and loan eligibility.

4

Vehicle Valuation (Used Car)

For used car loans, the vehicle undergoes inspection and valuation by the lender to determine the loan-to-value ratio.

5

Loan Approval & Agreement

Loan approved with sanctioned amount, interest rate, and EMI schedule. Loan agreement signed with full transparency on all terms.

6

Disbursement & Delivery

Loan amount disbursed directly to the dealer. Vehicle delivered to you. Drive your dream car home within days!
