Documents Required for Business Loan

KYC Documents (All Directors / Partners / Proprietor)

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card (Personal & Business PAN)
  • Passport Size Photos

Business Proof

  • GST Registration Certificate
  • Udyam Registration (MSME Certificate)
  • Trade License
  • Shop Establishment Certificate
  • Partnership Deed / MOA & AOA (for Pvt Ltd)

Financial Documents

  • Last 2–3 years Income Tax Returns
  • Last 2–3 years Balance Sheet
  • Profit & Loss Statement
  • GST Returns (6–12 months)
  • Last 6–12 months Bank Statement

Address Proof

  • Office Address Proof
  • Utility Bills

Business Loan Process – Step by Step

1

Loan Application

Business details submitted with loan requirement & purpose mentioned. Our team reviews your application and guides you through the initial process.

2

Financial Review

Comprehensive analysis including turnover check, GST verification, and bank transaction analysis to assess your business financial health.

3

Credit Assessment

Evaluation of business credit score and promoter CIBIL check to determine creditworthiness and loan eligibility.

4

Site Visit (if required)

Physical verification of business premises to validate operations and assess business authenticity.

5

Loan Sanction

Sanction letter issued with approved loan amount, interest rate, tenure, and repayment terms clearly mentioned.

6

Documentation & Agreement

Legal agreements signed with complete transparency. All terms and conditions explained in detail by our experts.

7

Disbursement

Funds transferred to business account. Usually takes 3–7 working days. Start growing your business immediately!
