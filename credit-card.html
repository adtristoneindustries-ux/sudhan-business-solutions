Documents Required for Credit Card Application

Identity Proof

  • PAN Card (Mandatory)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • Driving License

Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport
  • Utility Bills (Electricity/Water/Gas)
  • Rental Agreement
  • Property Tax Receipt

Income Proof (Salaried)

  • Last 3 Months Salary Slips
  • Last 6 Months Bank Statement
  • Form 16 / ITR
  • Employment Certificate
  • Appointment Letter

Income Proof (Self-Employed)

  • Last 2 Years ITR with Computation
  • Last 6 Months Bank Statement
  • Business Proof (GST/Shop License)
  • Profit & Loss Statement
  • Balance Sheet

Additional Documents

  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Credit Card Application Form
  • Existing Credit Card Statement (if any)
  • Office Address Proof

Credit Card Application Process – Step by Step

1

Choose Your Card

Select the credit card that best suits your lifestyle and spending needs. Our experts help you compare features, rewards, and benefits across different cards.

2

Check Eligibility

Verify your eligibility based on age (21-60 years), income criteria, employment status, and credit score. We guide you on improving eligibility if needed.

3

Submit Application

Fill out the credit card application form with accurate personal, employment, and financial details. Submit required documents for verification.

4

Document Verification

Bank verifies your identity proof, address proof, income documents, and employment details. Additional documents may be requested if needed.

5

Credit Assessment

Bank checks your CIBIL score, credit history, existing loans/credit cards, and repayment track record to assess creditworthiness.

6

Approval & Card Issuance

Once approved, credit card is issued with assigned credit limit. Card details sent via SMS/email. Physical card delivered to your address within 7-10 days.

7

Card Activation

Activate your credit card through SMS, phone banking, or mobile app. Set your PIN and start enjoying exclusive benefits and rewards!
