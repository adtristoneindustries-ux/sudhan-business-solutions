Govt Subsidy Loan Process – Step by Step

1

Identify the Right Scheme

Our experts help you identify the most suitable government subsidy scheme (PMEGP, MUDRA, CGTMSE, etc.) based on your business type and requirements.

2

Application Preparation

We assist in preparing your loan application with all required documents including project report, Udyam registration, and community certificates.

3

Bank Submission

Application submitted to the designated bank or financial institution along with the detailed project report and supporting documents.

4

Verification & Scrutiny

Bank verifies all documents, conducts site visit if required, and scrutinizes the project report for feasibility and eligibility.

5

Loan Sanction

Loan sanctioned with subsidy component clearly mentioned. Sanction letter issued with loan amount, interest rate, and repayment terms.

6

Disbursement & Subsidy Credit

Loan amount disbursed to your account. Government subsidy amount credited separately as per scheme guidelines. Start your business journey!
