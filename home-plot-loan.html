Home & Plot Loan Process – Step by Step

1

Property Selection

Identify your dream home or plot. Our team helps you understand the loan eligibility based on property value and your income profile.

2

Loan Application

Submit your application with KYC, income proof, and property documents. We compare offers from multiple banks to get you the best rate.

3

Income & Credit Assessment

Bank evaluates your CIBIL score, income stability, existing liabilities, and repayment capacity to determine the eligible loan amount.

4

Property Legal & Technical Verification

Bank's legal team verifies property documents and title deed. Technical team inspects and values the property to confirm loan-to-value ratio.

5

Loan Sanction

Sanction letter issued with approved loan amount, interest rate (fixed/floating), tenure, and EMI details clearly mentioned.

6

Agreement & Registration

Loan agreement and mortgage deed executed. Property registered in your name. Our team guides you through every legal step.

7

Disbursement

Loan amount disbursed to the seller or builder as per agreement. Move into your dream home and start your EMI journey!
